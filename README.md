# ParentConnect

A supportive digital community designed to bring stay-at-home parents together to share experiences, seek advice, and build meaningful connections.

## Overview

Many stay-at-home parents experience isolation, lack of support, and limited access to shared knowledge. ParentConnect addresses this by providing a centralized platform where parents can interact, learn from one another, and build a strong sense of community.

The platform combines social interaction, knowledge sharing, and smart features to create a safe and engaging environment for parents.


## Features

### User Profiles

* Register and log in securely
* Create and customize personal profiles
* Track activity and engagement

### Community Discussions

* Create posts and share experiences
* Comment and interact with other parents
* Like and react to posts

### Support Network

* Ask questions and get advice from other parents
* Share challenges and solutions
* Build meaningful connections

### Content Sharing

* Share text-based stories and tips
* Upload images (optional)
* Save or bookmark helpful posts

### Smart Assistance (Optional AI)

* Get parenting tips and suggestions
* Personalized recommendations based on activity

### Notifications

* Get updates on replies, likes, and interactions
* Stay engaged with community activity
* 

## Tech Stack 

* Frontend: Flutter (Mobile)
* Backend: Python (Django/Flask) / PHP / Spring Boot
* Database: MySQL
* Authentication: Firebase Auth

## Goals

* Reduce isolation among stay-at-home parents
* Encourage knowledge sharing and peer support
* Build a safe and inclusive online community
* Provide easy access to parenting resources

## Problem Statement

Stay-at-home parents often lack a strong support system and may feel disconnected from others facing similar experiences. Existing platforms are either too broad or not tailored to their specific needs, making it difficult to find relatable advice and community.
